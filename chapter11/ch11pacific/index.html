Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California North Coast with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay at the Pacific Trails Resort includes a sumptuously appointed private yurt and a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

  • Special Lodging: Enjoy panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean on California's North Coast.
  • Private Yurt: Comfort and luxury in our unique yurts amidst nature.
  • Cooked Breakfast: Fresh and delicious breakfast prepared each morning.
  • Endless Adventure: Explore nature between ocean and forest.
