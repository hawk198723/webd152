Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Gentle Hatha Yoga focuses on slow and gentle movements, making it suitable for all fitness levels. It promotes relaxation, flexibility, and stress relief through a series of postures and breathing exercises.
Vinyasa Yoga
Vinyasa Yoga is a dynamic style of yoga that emphasizes flowing movements and synchronized breath. It involves a series of poses that seamlessly transition from one to another, promoting strength, flexibility, and mindfulness.
Restorative Yoga
Restorative Yoga is a deeply relaxing and rejuvenating practice that involves holding supported poses for an extended period. It aims to promote deep relaxation, release tension, and restore balance in the body and mind.
