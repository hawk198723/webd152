Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California North Coast with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay at the Pacific Trails Resort includes a sumptuously appointed private yurt and a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

  • Experience Special Lodging: On the beautiful North Coast of California, we offer a unique lodging experience that allows you to enjoy panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean.
  • Luxurious Private Yurt: Your stay at Pacific Trails Resort includes a sumptuously appointed private yurt, offering comfort and luxury while you enjoy nature.
  • Cooked-to-Order Breakfast: Your stay at Pacific Trails Resort includes a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning, prepared fresh and delicious by our chef according to your tastes.
  • Infinite Exploration: Nestled between ocean and forest, our resort offers endless nature adventures, catering to beach or woodland enthusiasts alike.
