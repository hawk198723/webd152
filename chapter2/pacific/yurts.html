The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
A yurt is a round, portable dwelling that has been used by nomadic cultures of Central Asia for over three thousand years. These structures are known for their durability, comfort, and ability to be easily disassembled and transported.
How are the yurts finished?
Our yurts are beautifully finished with hardwood floors and fine-quality wooden
What Should I Bring?
We recommend you bring casual clothing suitable for outdoor activities, personal toiletries, and any specialty items that will make your stay more enjoyable. However, rest assured that all of our yurts come fully equipped with bedding, kitchen essentials, and a selection of toiletries.
